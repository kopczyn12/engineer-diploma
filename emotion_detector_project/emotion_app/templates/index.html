About the Project

EmotionLab was birthed as an engineer's diploma project designed to innovatively detect human emotions in videos and photos. Combining advanced technology with user-focused design, it's a perfect fusion of academic insight and real-world application.

About the Author

Michał Kopczyński, a Machine Learning and Software Engineer at aidmed.ai, has a profound interest in AI. EmotionLab is the crowning achievement of his FER research and serves as his engineering diploma project from Gdańsk University of Technology.
